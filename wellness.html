Student Wellness

GIBC hosts various wellness events throughout the year for students to come together and discuss any topics regarding research, academics, current events, career goals, and much much more.

Wellness Group Discussions

Every quarter, students gather to share personal experiences and concerns while providing others advice and support.

Monthly Anti-racism Book Club

In collaboration with Biomedical Sciences and Diversity and Science Lecture Series (DASL), we hold monthly book clubs to explore issues of racism in America as a means of better understanding ourselves, our history, and our community.
